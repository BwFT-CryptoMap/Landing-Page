About CryptoMap

Mission

CryptoMap is an interactive heat map that visualizes the current market value and volume of practically any relatively well-known cryptocurrency out there, including Bitcoin, Ethereum, Litecoin, NEO and many more. Based on your preference, the tool enables you to monitor any changes in the market value and volume during the last hour, day and week.
